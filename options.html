Change log

v3.0
- Added welcome message overlay with instructions that appears on first activation.
- Added drag handle at the top of highlight boxes to move them without resizing.
- Added Exit button alongside Clear button for easy extension deactivation.
- Added Show Pony logo and branding in top left corner.
- Improved button spacing and positioning to be responsive to text changes.
- Enhanced visual styling with gradient borders and rounded corners.
- Fixed image loading for proper logo display in content scripts.

v2.0
- Corners of the highlighted box are now draggable for easier adjustments without redrawing.
- Added corner indicators and cursor style to indicate draggability.
- Added a Clear button in the top right that resets the overlay without turning off the extension.

v1.0
- Initial release of Show Pony.
- Highlight important parts of your page by clicking and dragging over the area of focus.
- A semi-transparent overlay blocks out everything else.
- Useful for screen sharing during video meetings or even in person when all looking at a screen.
